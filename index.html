Soft Matter Lab

Our research focuses on the study of soft matter systems, including rheology and microrheology.

Tissue Engineering Lab

We investigate the development of tissue scaffolds, including 3D bioprinting and cell culture techniques.

Biophotonics Lab

Our research explores the application of light-based technologies, including optical tweezers and spectroscopy.

Computational Biophysics

We use computational models to simulate and analyze biological systems, including RT-PCR and AFM data analysis.
